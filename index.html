FOUNDER

Alex Agrasanchez boasts over 40 years of executive experience in the TV and Film industry, demonstrating expertise in Television, Programming acquisitions, TV & Film Production, Business development, and Distribution.

His proficiency extends to strategic negotiations, film production, acquisitions, and sales. In 1985, he assumed control of Cine Virgo following his late brother Rene, embarking on the production of successful Spanish and English-speaking films. Establishing Movie Mx International, Inc. in 1992, Agrasanchez helmed his own distribution company, specializing in aiding enterprises in acquiring rights to classic films and effectively monetizing content in both standard and digital eras. His initiatives resulted in numerous multi-million-dollar licenses globally.
